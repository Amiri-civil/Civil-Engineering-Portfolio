# Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) in Iran

## 📌 Overview

This study investigates the role of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) in road transportation and evaluates the implementation of ITS in Iran up to the year 1400.

## 📝 Publication

* Journal: Civil and Project Journal (CPJ)
* Type: Research Article
* Year: 2022
* DOI: https://doi.org/10.22034/cpj.2023.04.03.1133
* Indexed in Google Scholar

## 📊 Research Focus

* Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)
* Road Transportation
* Traffic Management and Safety
* Environmental Impacts of Transportation

## 📈 Key Findings

* Reduction in air pollution (e.g., up to 25% in Sydney)
* Reduction in fuel consumption (e.g., 10% in Paris)
* Improved traffic monitoring and management
* Implementation of ITS tools in Iran (e.g., traffic counters, weigh-in-motion systems, surveillance systems)
